About Me

Hello there! I'm Amanda Gomez, a passionate programmer and book enthusiast. Welcome to my book blog, where I share the books that have played a crucial role in shaping my journey as a programmer. Throughout my learning process, I have come across numerous valuable resources that have helped me grow as a developer, and I believe that sharing these gems can inspire and guide others on their path to becoming better programmers too. In addition to programming, I love working on small projects just for fun. It's a wonderful way to experiment, learn new things, and let my creativity flow. I'm excited to use this platform to showcase some of these exciting projects and share my experiences with you. As I continue to explore the vast world of programming and immerse myself in inspiring literature, I'll be continuously adding to the list of books that have impacted me. So, stay tuned for regular updates and recommendations as my knowledge and time grow. Together, let's embark on a journey of continuous learning and discovery! Happy reading and coding!
